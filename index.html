ABOUT US

Established in 2023, Schools Division of Ilocos Norte has left a mark not only in academics but also in sports in such a short span of time. Be it in athletics our any other form of game, the energy and ethusiasm shown by the students of SDO Ilocos Norte has impressed one and all. The passion to compete among ourselves is the fuel which keeps us going. To harness and develop this energy, several sporting events are timely organised in the elementary and secondary. Be it football, basketball, badminton, cricket, table tennis or any other form of game, we at SDO Ilocos Norte always strive to give the best and keep up the good name. INAA Meet 2024 is one such event designed in keeping such ideas in mind.
